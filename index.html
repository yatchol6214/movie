1/2 Life Online

What is life? Are we alive? Is there a meaning of life? We don't know either! But in the movie, "1/2 Life online", we use the cutting edge of science and technology, "The Nerve Gear", created by "Toby Weeb Miyazaki" to try to solve some of the age long philosophy questions. The Nerve gear transfers your memory and consciousness into a machine. We use this technology to our advantage, and use it to experiment on humans like never before. We can unethically test, the human response to an extreme situations, and see how they will react, and also other theories that has never been able to be tested due to it's high risk.

Toby Miyazaki, head director: Tired of working for your harsh boss,doing the same thing everyday,getting minimum wage? Daydreaming in the office imagining you are one of the most famous youTuber getting pay to play/stream games.Now you have the chance to get pay well while playing games.You will be rewarded with $100,000,000 for beating the game. Be caution you will only have one life in this game, once you die in the game, you will actually die in real life. You go big or you go home.What's the point of working like a cow when you could be a billionaire?
